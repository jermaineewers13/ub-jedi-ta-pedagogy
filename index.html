Justice, Equity, Diversity & Inclusion TA Pedagogy

Office Hours
Common Issue #1: Students reluctant to discuss personal challenges.

Solution #1: Offer anonymous sign-up and optional breakout rooms to encourage sharing.

Common Issue #2: Scheduling conflicts in diverse time zones.

Solution #2: Rotate office hour times and record sessions for asynchronous access.

Lectures
Inclusive language and diverse examples.

Use case studies from varied backgrounds and pronounce names correctly to show respect.

Active learning with breakouts.

Mix groups randomly and assign roles to ensure balanced participation.

Multiple content formats (text, audio, visuals).

Provide transcripts, slide decks, and captioned videos for accessibility.

Grading
Rubric-based grading with clear criteria.

Publish rubrics ahead of time and align feedback to each criterion.

Point-by-point feedback for transparency.

Use inline comments and summary notes to explain deductions.

Revision opportunities to support learning.

Allow one resubmission per assignment with incremental grading.

Additional Practices
Regular student feedback surveys.

Use anonymous polls to adjust teaching strategies in real-time.

Collaborative syllabus co-creation.

Host a kickoff session for students to suggest topics and grading policies.

Peer-mentoring study groups.

Pair experienced students with newcomers and track progress milestones.
